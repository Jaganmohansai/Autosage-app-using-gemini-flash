# Customer Problem Statement

## Overview
This document outlines the customer problem statement using the visual template shown above.

## User Persona 1: Vehicle Buyer (Two-wheeler or Four-wheeler)
### Problem:
- Trying to get complete details about a vehicle such as brand, model, mileage, features, and price
- Vehicle information is spread across many websites and takes a lot of time to search
- Most platforms show generic or incomplete details and do not give instant insights from images

### Emotion: Confused and unsure about making the right vehicle choice

---

## User Persona 2: Student or Common User Interested in Vehicles
### Problem:
- Wants to understand vehicle specifications quickly using images
- Reading long articles and specifications is boring and time-consuming
- There is no simple AI-based tool that explains vehicle details in an easy format

### Emotion: Frustrated and less confident in understanding vehicle specifications

---

## User Persona 3: Eco-Conscious User
### Problem:
- Wants to find fuel-efficient or eco-friendly vehicles
- Information about mileage, resale value, and efficiency is not clearly available
- Most platforms do not summarize vehicle details in one place

### Emotion: Unsatisfied and overwhelmed while choosing an eco-friendly vehicle

---

## Solution
The AutoSage App addresses these problems by:
1. Providing instant vehicle details from images
2. Using AI (Gemini Flash) to generate comprehensive vehicle information
3. Making vehicle research quick, easy, and accessible to all user types
